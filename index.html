Finding Joy In Cooking Again

Over the last few years, I have lost the joy in food and cooking due to major depression. I lost sight of who I am and what makes me happy. In this journey or learning web development.

I have found joy in many aspects in my life again. Obviously a big one is that I just love to learn. Learning HTML and building these projects have been so fun.

Watching and cooking food has been re-introduced into my life without guilt. Although my weekly cooking will be still bulk, bro meals. I intend to actually "cook" a meal once a week.

It's possible that this commitment to cooking once a week will also help restore relationships in my life. Follow along my journey as a self-taught web developer and finding joy in life.
