Egg Sando Filling

Description

I have chosen the Egg Sando Filling recipe from the one and only Doobydobap. A delicious creamy and savoury mixture of eggs, kewpie mayo and some S&P.Egg sandos filling is a reocurring staple in my breakfasts. The reason I say the filling itself is a staple and not just a sando is because I often rotate the carbs that go along the egg sando filling. Typically alternating between rice, toast, naan, and sometimnes just straight up filling. That stuff is bomb af.

Ingredients

  • 6 ten-minute boiled eggs (4 eggs + 2 egg yolk only)
  • 1 raw egg yolk
  • 1 seven-minute boiled eggs for garnish (optional)
  • 2 tbsp japanese mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 morning bbang or white bread

Steps

  1. Place 7 eggs in room temperature water and heat on high.
  2. The cooking time begins once the water starts boiliing. Make sure to take out 1 of the eggs at 7 minute mark if you want to garnish on top.
  3. Place immediately in an ice bath and let the eggs cool completely before peeling.
  4. Dice the eggs into small cubes and add the rest of the ingredients.
  5. Quarter the 7 minute egg for garnish. Enjoy!
